ABOUT US AND OUR MISSION

weCare Home Nursing Service

Our mission at weCare HomeCare and Nursing Services is to help people and their families bring healthcare into the home. We do this by finding and managing in-home health care, assisted living services, and other domestic support. From health assessment and recruitment strategy and payroll solutions to placement and provision of ongoing homecare support services, weCare HomeCare is with you every step of the way, at every challenge and every stage of life.

Child care, nannies, housekeepers and companions
Part-time to 24-hr care, live-in or live-out
Elder care and special needs support



Company Profile

weCare is a combination of a placement agency and a home support agency, but with a few important differences. We feel that we are unlike a typical placement agency in that we screen and qualify all potential caregivers for our clients based on their specific needs, and we’re there from first interview through your 90-day guarantee period , and beyond (contact us for details). This allows us to maintain a very high placement success rate.


We are different than home support agencies because once we’ve found you a caregiver, you can work with them directly. We will only provide you added services and support at your request – otherwise, once you’ve paid your placement and any related processing fees, you won’t receive another bill from weCare. weCare brings over 30 years of combined experience in the health care and placement fields to our clients across Ghana. It is our goal to be the leading provider of homecare professionals in West Africa and beyond.
