WECARE AND NURSING SERVICES

weCare can provide as little or as much senior care for elder and ill clients as desired, or as required by family members and legal guardians.




24-Hour Care

We can provide around-the-clock monitoring and personal care for senior clients unable to live independently. As part of any assessment and recruitment efforts, we will work with the client to develop a care plan that takes into account the care requirements, caregiver profiles and employment standards.




Standard Care

Many clients may not require live-in care, but do need daily visits and support from a caregiver to ensure meal schedules are being met, and sufficient levels of exercise, stimulation and interaction are being maintained. In some cases, the objective is companionship; Simplified HomeCare can provide a variety of experienced caregivers for all personality types and preferences.




Temporary/Respite Care

On an as-needed basis, Simplified HomeCare can provide access to experienced caregivers for a variety of needs with seniors.




Live-In/Live-Out Nanny

A popular outcome of the federal government’s Temporary Foreign Worker Program, families in need of experienced child care and household support can directly employ a nanny (minimum 40 hours per week) who is willing to be live-in.

For families who don’t require full-time care, or are unable to accommodate a live-in caregiver for any reason, live-out nannies provide opportunities for flexible child care options, such as after-school care and contracted babysitting.




Household Support

Many caregivers bring a host of skills to the home, and can support basic household needs, such as:

    • 1. Cooking and meal preparation
      2. Cleaning and basic household maintenance
      3. Driving, carpooling and errands




    • Document Support

    If you are looking to process a caregiver through the Temporary Foreign Worker Program – from overseas or locally – we provide support services to help you assemble all required forms. If you require immigration support, we can help coordinate legal and immigration services with our affiliate law firm Maynard Kischer Stojicevic. Please contact us and we can start the process from the beginning, or take over where you left off.
