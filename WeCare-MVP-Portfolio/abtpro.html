REASONS FOR THIS PROJECT

Inspitations for this project

What lead me to this project was about the death of my granny.

It happened on the 11th of April 2011, She was ill and our care as broken down and health care givers were on strike as well. So we could not get her to the hospital, all because of the circumstances and she passed on. Based on that i always wanted a platform that could bring care givers and clients even closer for easier access regardless on the state of the nation. and this is a protfolio project for holberton school Link to the Project
